Site Name:Seclusive Utah

The purpose of this site is to provide information on locations and availability for lesser known recreation areas and activities in the state of Utah for those who truly want to escape from the bustle and crowding of better known and visited sites; such as national parks.

Site Purpose:

The site will provide driving directions to these remote sites. Links to register for their use where available. And images of and a summary of recreational activities: hiking, fishing, biking, water skiing, backpacking, etc. that a suitable/available in the nearby region. The home page will offer an introduction to the site and its purpose, as well as offer a signup form for visitors to receive a monthly new letter. A secondary page will list the various locations' details as described above; separated by region. A third, contact page, will allow site users a secondary form with which they can ask questions, update info on suggested areas and activities, and suggest new areas not already listed on the site.

Color Schema:

The primary color used for this site will be #355E3B, suitable for the theme of camping. It will be used on the header, h1, and footer elements; along with white text to allow sufficent contrast.
The secondary color used for this site will be #F3FFC6, a softer color creating a suitable contract for other content, such as the backgrounds of sections and paragraphs.
A possible third color will be #B37942.It will be used for subheadings: h2, & h3. Text colors used for the header, header elements, and footer will be white. Black will be used to contrast with the Secondary color #F3FFC6.
