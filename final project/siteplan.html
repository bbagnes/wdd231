Site Name: Seclusive Utah

The purpose of this site is to provide information on locations and availability for lesser known recreation areas and activities in the state of Utah for those who truly want to escape from the bustle and crowding of better known and visited sites; such as national parks.

Site Purpose:

The site will provide driving directions to these remote recreational sites. Links to register for their use where available. And images of and a summary of recreational activities: hiking, fishing, biking, water skiing, backpacking, etc. that a suitable/available in the nearby region. The home page will offer an introduction to the site and its purpose. A secondary page will list the various locations' details as described above; separated by region. A third, Join Us page, will offer a signup form for visitors to receive a monthly newsletter; as well as, ask questions, update info on suggested areas and activities, and suggest new areas not already listed on the site.

Scenarios:

  • Where are good places in Utah to get away from the crowds and hustle of society and enjoy nature?
  • What types of recreational activities can I find and enjoy in these less used and isolated recreational areas?
  • What can I do to help keep these areas pristine?

Color Schema:

The primary color used for this site will be #355E3B, suitable for the theme of camping. It will be used on the header, h1, and footer elements; along with white text to allow sufficent contrast.
The secondary color used for this site will be #B37942.It will be used for subheadings: h2, & h3.
An accent color, #F3FFC6, will be used for other content, such as the backgrounds of sections and paragraphs.
Text colors used for the header, header elements, and footer will be white. Black will be used to contrast with the accent color #F3FFC6.
